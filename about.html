About Suntrik

Suntrik is a trusted solar energy solutions provider committed to accelerating India’s transition toward clean, reliable, and sustainable power. We combine deep EPC experience with project execution excellence and customer-focused innovation.

At a Glance

Built on the strengths of Suntrik Solutions (est. 2018) and Suntrik Green Pvt. Ltd. (inc. 2024), we provide end-to-end services — advisory, engineering, turnkey EPC and O&M — for commercial, industrial and rural projects across North India and beyond.
